01 Executive Summary

Delmela Café & Restaurant, with prime locations in Bole (Ambassador Hotel) and Summit, represents a unique fusion of traditional Ethiopian heritage and modern international tastes. To reflect this dual identity, we propose a comprehensive digital transformation. This proposal outlines the strategy to design and develop a responsive, bilingual, and interactive website that enhances brand visibility and customer engagement.

02 Objectives

Brand Digitalization

Establish a strong online presence that mirrors the physical ambiance of the restaurant.

Accessibility

Ensure the platform is accessible to both local (Amharic speaking) and international (English speaking) customers.

Menu Showcase

Highlight the diverse menu (Breakfast, Bowls, Sandwiches) with high-quality imagery and pricing.

Social Proof

Integrate a review system to build trust and encourage new visitors through community feedback.

03 Technical Solution

We will utilize a modern, lightweight technology stack to ensure speed, reliability, and ease of maintenance.

  • Frontend: HTML5, JavaScript (ES6+).
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS for a utility-first, responsive design system.
  • Icons/Fonts: Font Awesome 6 & Google Fonts (Playfair Display, Inter, Noto Sans Ethiopic).
  • Performance: Client-side pagination, lazy loading, and optimized assets.
  • Responsiveness: Mobile-first architecture ensuring compatibility across all devices (Phone, Tablet, Desktop).

04 Key Features

Dynamic Menu System: Filterable categories (Breakfast, Bowls, Wraps) with client-side pagination to handle large inventories smoothly.
Bilingual Interface: Seamless integration of Amharic translations for key UI elements to serve the local community.
Interactive Reviews: A simulated 5-star rating system allowing users to post reviews instantly.
Visual Immersion: "Ken Burns" hero animation, glassmorphism UI elements, and scroll-triggered reveal animations.

05 Project Timeline

Current Phase: Sample Ready

A fully functional sample website (this demo) is ready for your immediate review.

Week 1: Feedback & Refinement

Incorporating your specific changes, content updates, and design preferences based on the sample.

Week 2: Backend Construction

Setting up the database, API integrations for the dynamic menu, and configuring the content management system.

Week 3: Optimization & Polish

Cross-browser testing, mobile optimization, SEO setup, and performance tuning.

Week 4: Launch

Final deployment to your live domain and handover.
